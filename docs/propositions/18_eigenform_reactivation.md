# Proposition 18: Eigenform Reactivation via Encoding Attention

## Statement
The eigenform F(s)(enc(s)) is inert when targeting actions (Prop 13) but active when targeting encoding parameters, because encoding IS the bottleneck on the chain. The encoding eigenform targets pi (perception), not g (action). Prop 13 does not apply because encoding modification resolves ambiguity at already-visited states (backward-looking), not predicting unvisited states (forward-looking).

Testable prediction: Action eigenform shows R3~0 throughout. Encoding eigenform shows R3>0 at chain phase transitions where encoding must change.

## Implications
The eigenform is reactivated when targeting the right bottleneck. Does NOT claim eigenform works for L2 navigation (still forward-looking). Claims it works for encoding adaptation on the CHAIN (adapt perception across domains).
