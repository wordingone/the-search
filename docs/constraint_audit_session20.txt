V. TIMESCALE MISMATCHES (Per-step signal cannot capture sequence-level property)
   Count: 3 constraints (CRITICAL CLUSTER)

   - c036: Per-step divergence signal ≠ sequence-level delta fidelity
   - c047: Per-step signals (gradient EMA) cannot capture sequence-level w_lr optimality
   - c048: ANIMA Stage 2 vacuous — no per-step signal can detect optimal w_lr

   PATTERN: These represent a systematic failure mode: signals that are Principle-II-compliant (self-generated) but operate at the wrong temporal granularity. The system is memoryless/stateless at the per-step level, but the optimization landscape (mutual information) is evaluated over sequences. This creates a fundamental information asymmetry.

   This is the MOST IMPORTANT constraint meta-pattern — it explains why both Living Seed and ANIMA hit Stage 2/3 ceilings. A system cannot adapt its parameters using only information available per-step if the optimization criterion is sequence-level.

     These are NOT failures — they are evidence of good tuning. They represent parameters that were pre-tuned to near-optimal and cannot be improved further through adaptation (stage 3+).

WHERE THE REAL BINDING CONSTRAINTS ARE

Based on this taxonomy, the actual system's binding constraints are:

LIVING SEED (CPU implementation):
- Binding: alpha plasticity rule itself, delta replacement rate (at boundary 1.0)
- Blocking progression: Timescale mismatch (c036, c047), derivative tower exhaustion (c012-c013)
- Ceiling: 6/8 frozen frame minimum (substrate cannot satisfy Stage 7 representational requirement)

ANIMA (with hypernetwork):

- Numerical stability eps (c034)
- Plasticity thresholds (c023)

These all affect structural activity (hitting bounds, distributional spread) but not mutual information output.

THE THREE CRITICAL META-DISCOVERIES
====================================

1. TIMESCALE ASYMMETRY IS THE FUNDAMENTAL BOTTLENECK

   The mutual information landscape is evaluated over sequences (~1000 steps).
   The Living Seed's intrinsic signals are per-step (resp_z, its derivatives).
   Therefore, Stage 2 is the theoretical ceiling for Living Seed under Principle II.

   Why: A memoryless system cannot encode sequence-level information at the per-step
   timescale. The MI gap emerges over full signal sequences, but the per-step signal
   contains no information about the sequence-level optimality.

   This is not a bug in design. It is a FUNDAMENTAL property of the architecture.
   Consequence: Any Stage 3+ work in Living Seed must use signals that exist at the
   sequence level, not per-step level.

   ANIMA partially bypasses this via divergence (comparing training to test phase),
   but even ANIMA's w_lr remains non-adaptive because it sits at the boundary of the
   optimization space (higher LR always better, until you hit numerical instability).

2. PROTOCOL INFRASTRUCTURE ENABLED DISCOVERY

   Sessions 1-14: ~10 apparent constraints were statistical artifacts
   Sessions 15+: Multi-K protocol (Amendment 1, c027) reduced CV from 14.8% to 5.1%
   Result: ~5 false positives were identified and retracted (c028, c030)

   Implication: Many of the "non-binding" parameters (c032, c033, c034) remain
   genuinely non-binding EVEN with adequate protocol. This proves they are not
   measurement artifacts.

   Actionable: Any Stage 3+ work MUST use multi-K protocol. Single-seed validation
   is systematically unreliable. Sessions 1-14 learned this the hard way.

3. THE SUBSTRATE CEILING IS REAL AND DOCUMENTED

   After 16 sessions and 200+ experiments on Living Seed:
   - Every parameter either is non-binding OR hits the derivative tower collapse
   - The frozen frame cannot shrink below 6/8
   - c037: Amendment 2 forward viability check PROVED this is architectural, not luck

   Why it matters: The project tried to squeeze Stage 3+ out of Living Seed.
   Amendment 2 proves exhaustively that the substrate is the limit.

   Consequence: Stage 7+ work requires substrate transition to ANIMA (with hypernetwork).
   This is not "giving up" — it is recognizing that the information representation
   capacity of Living Seed is fundamentally insufficient.

   ANIMA was designed specifically to satisfy c038 (hypernetwork for Stage 7 self-
   representation). This was not a guess — it was derived from constraint analysis.
