# Ban Policy

Bans remove mechanism families from the search. Each ban specifies WHAT is banned and WHY (in its own file). Lift conditions are universal — they apply to ALL bans equally.

## Lifting Condition

When the search is genuinely paralyzed under accumulated constraints and no existing literature or known methods provides a viable path forward, bans may be selectively lifted in controlled combinations to map the feasible region through recombination.

No single ban is lifted in isolation. The purpose of lifting is controlled recombination — testing specific mechanism combinations against findings from the constrained search.
